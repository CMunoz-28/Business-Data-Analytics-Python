## Analytical Approach

Across projects, the analytical workflow follows industry best practices:

1. Problem framing and KPI definition
2. Data cleaning and exploratory analysis
3. Statistical testing and modeling
4. Model validation and robustness checks
5. Interpretation of results in a business context

This approach reflects the role of a **modern business analyst** as a bridge between **technical analysis and strategic decision-making** .
