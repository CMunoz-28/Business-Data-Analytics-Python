# Business Analytics with Python

**Professional Projects Portfolio**

## Featured Work

### [RFM Customer Segmentation](./Segmentation/RFM)
Segments customers by Recency, Frequency, and Monetary value to identify high-value, at-risk, and dormant audiences. Produces actionable segments directly applicable to retention and re-engagement campaigns.

### [Google Causal Impact — Marketing Intervention Analysis](./Econometrics%20and%20Causal%20Inference/Google%20Causal%20Impact)
Uses Bayesian structural time-series modeling to isolate the true causal lift of a marketing intervention — separating real impact from trend, seasonality, and noise. Goes beyond correlation to answer *did this campaign actually work?*

### [Gaussian Mixture Model Clustering](./Segmentation/Gaussian%20Mixture%20Model)
Applies probabilistic clustering to a credit card customer dataset, assigning soft segment probabilities that capture natural ambiguity in customer behavior — more realistic than hard k-means assignments.

## Overview

This repository contains business analytics projects developed in Python, based on the Udemy course [*Business Data Analyst — Learn Python, Statistics, and Data Analytics*](https://www.udemy.com/course/business-data-analytics-intelligence-with-python/). The work emphasizes **practical, business-oriented analytics**, combining statistical foundations, predictive modeling, causal inference, and segmentation to answer real-world business questions.

## Skills Demonstrated

* **Descriptive & Inferential Statistics** — mean, variance, confidence intervals, hypothesis testing
* **Data Cleaning & Preparation** — Pandas-based analytical workflows
* **Regression Modeling** — linear, multilinear, and logistic regression
* **Model Evaluation** — R², MAE, RMSE, confusion matrix, AUC, F1-score
* **Causal Inference** — Google Causal Impact, propensity score matching, difference-in-differences logic
* **Customer Segmentation** — RFM scoring, Gaussian Mixture Models
* **Machine Learning** — Random Forest, feature importance, model tuning
* **Time Series & Forecasting** — Facebook Prophet, trend and seasonality decomposition
* **Business Interpretation** — translating analytical results into decision-ready insights
